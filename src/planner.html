Bloc Planner by Humphrey

This is a tool designed to help plan your game in the massively multiplayer online nation simulator game BLOC. It can be used to calculate growth and things like the next time you can afford a factory, or what will happen if you conscript three times. It cannot predict other players behaviour, the game may change and many formulae may be aproximations so it may not be 100% accurate. Get started by clicking settings and inputing values from your nation.
